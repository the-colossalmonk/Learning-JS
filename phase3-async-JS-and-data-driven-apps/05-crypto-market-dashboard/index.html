Glossary & Info

  • Market Cap: The total value of a cryptocurrency. (Current Price x Circulating Supply).
  • 24h Change: The percentage a coin's price has moved up or down in the last 24 hours.
  • Sparkline: A small line chart showing the general price trend over the last 7 days.
